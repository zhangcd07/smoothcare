About Dr. Chengda Zhang

I am Dr. Chengda Zhang, an experienced internist and intensivist. My journey in medicine began at Peking Union Medical College, Tsinghua University, where I received my MD degree. Following this, I completed my training in internal medicine at Beaumont Health in Michigan and further specialized in critical care medicine at Oregon Health & Science University.

In addition to my medical degrees, I hold a degree in clinical informatics, blending my medical expertise with the advancements in healthcare technology. My career spans across various hospital ICUs on the west coast, where I care for patients in critical conditions.

My focus areas include health planning, health risk mitigation, recognizing and managing urgent conditions, post-ICU care. I am known for my efficiency and dedication to meeting patients' needs as promptly as possible.

My practice philosophy is rooted in using my knowledge and expertice to help patients achieve their life goals. For me, patient satisfaction is the top priority, and I strive to provide care that aligns with their individual needs and expectations.
