At SmoothCare, we specialize in providing top-tier telemedicine services. Our primary focus includes:

    We offer a wide range of primary care services, focused on maintaining health and preventing disease, such as:

    • Health Planning: Personalized health assessments and long-term wellness planning.
    • Cancer Risk Mitigation: Early detection strategies, lifestyle counseling, and monitoring for signs of cancer.
    • Cardiovascular Risk Mitigation: Heart health assessments, cholesterol management, and hypertension control.

    Ensuring seamless healthcare, especially after hospital discharge, is crucial. Our continuity of care services include:

    • Post-Discharge Planning: Coordinating care plans to ensure a smooth transition from hospital to home.
    • Follow-up Planning: Prioritizing and scheduling necessary follow-up appointments and check-ups.
    • Specialist Coordination: Facilitating communication with specialists to ensure comprehensive care management.

    Our urgent care services are equipped to handle a variety of conditions, particularly those common among the elderly, such as:

    • Urinary Tract Infections (UTIs): Prompt care and management.
    • Respiratory Infections: Including bronchitis, pneumonia, and influenza.
    • Cellulitis: Treatment of skin infections and inflammation.
    • Arthritis Flare-ups: Managing sudden pain and swelling in joints.
    • Gastrointestinal Issues: Addressing gastritis, constipation, and other digestive problems.
    • Headaches or Migraines: Assessment and relief for persistent or severe headaches.
